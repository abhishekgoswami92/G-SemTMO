LocHDR LR catalog - Folder consists of Lightroom enhancement catalogs for the Local HDR dataset. 
The selected 781 image names (from MIT-Adobe FiveK) can be found in the 'Data\Preprocessed Dataset\Namelists\781_names_LocHDR.npy' file.
The first 100/781 images correspond to '100 images.lrcat' while the last 681/781 images correspond to the '681 images.lrcat' files.

The HC200 dataset is a subset of LocHDR. The selected 200 image names can be found in the 
'Data\Preprocessed Dataset\Namelists\200_names_HC200.npy' file.
